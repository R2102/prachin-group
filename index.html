About Prachin Chemical Inc.

Prachin Chemical Inc. is the U.S.-based distribution arm of Prachin Pharmachem, a globally recognized manufacturer of pharmaceutical excipients, nutraceutical ingredients, functional mineral salts and APIs. Operating from the United States, we serve as a reliable sourcing partner for formulators and manufacturers across North America.

Backed by over 30 years of manufacturing expertise, Prachin Chemical Inc. ensures timely and compliant delivery of pharmacopeia-grade ingredients, including cellulose derivatives, citrates, stearates, and co-processed excipients. Our materials conform to global standards such as IP, BP, USP, Ph.Eur., and JP, and are supported by certifications including GMP, ISO, HALAL, KOSHER, Excipact, and USDMF filings.

We are committed to supplying consistent, regulatory-compliant ingredients with responsive local support—empowering formulation success across the pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, and food sectors.
