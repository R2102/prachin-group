Overview

Croscarmellose Sodium (PARCROS) is a crosslinked sodium carboxymethyl cellulose used as a superdisintegrant in a wide range of oral dosage forms. It offers efficient disintegration at low concentrations through its excellent wicking and swelling properties. Available in multiple grades, PARCROS is suitable for direct compression, wet, and dry granulation, and is widely used in both pharmaceutical and nutraceutical tablets, capsules, ODTs, blends, and granules.

Features

  • Exhibits rapid disintegration and enhanced drug release profiles
  • Demonstrates superior swelling capacity, leading to faster dissolution rates compared to traditional disintegrants
  • Offers excellent compatibility and stability with a broad range of active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs)
  • Highly effective at low concentrations, minimizing excipient load
  • Ideal for use in direct compression, wet granulation, and various tableting processes

Applications

Pharmaceutical

Widely used as the primary disintegrant in a multitude of solid oral dosage forms, including tablets and capsules

Nutraceutical

Enhances the disintegration and dissolution of dietary supplements formulated as tablets or capsules

Food

Potential applications may include thickening and stabilizing functions (subject to regional regulations)
